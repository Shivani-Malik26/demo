Virat and Anushka

Indian batting star Virat Kohli and Anushka Sharma have announced the birth of their second child, a baby boy named Akaay on February 20, Tuesday. In a statement released on both their social media profiles, Kohli and Anushka said that the boy was born on 15th February.
